# DataBridge Hub Setup Guide

This guide walks you through setting up your DataBridge Hub and connecting it to your smart meter.

## Before You Begin

Gather the following items:

- DataBridge Hub
- Included power adapter
- Your Wi-Fi network name and password
- A phone or tablet with the EnergyGrid App installed
- Your EnergyGrid account credentials

## Sign In to the EnergyGrid App

1. **Open the App**

   Tap **Get Started** on the welcome screen.

2. **Enter Your Credentials**

   Enter your EnergyGrid account email and password to sign in.

3. **Reset Your Password if Needed**

   Tap **Forgot Password?** at the bottom of the sign-in screen to reset your password.

> **Note:** Use the email address associated with your EnergyGrid account as your username.

## Navigate the App

The navigation bar at the bottom of the screen provides access to the main features:

| Section | Description |
|:--|:--|
| **Home** | Track your progress against energy goals and view alerts when on-peak hours approach. |
| **Advisor** | View personalized data and tips to save energy and reduce costs. |
| **Usage** | View your home's energy usage in real time. |
| **Toolkit** | Manage smart home devices, set automation rules, and check DataBridge Hub connectivity. |
| **Menu** | View your plan, settings, energy tips, and help resources. |

## Access Help and Support

1. **Open the Menu**

   Tap the **Menu** icon in the navigation bar.

2. **Find Articles and Guides**

   Select **Help Center** to browse app features and how-to guides.

3. **Contact Support**

   Select **Support Center** to start a live chat or find the support phone number and email address.

## Set Up Your DataBridge Hub

### Find the Right Location

1. **Choose a Spot Near Your Meter**

   Place the DataBridge Hub within 30 feet of your smart meter. Avoid placing it near routers or large metal objects such as refrigerators.

2. **Have Your Wi-Fi Password Ready**

   You'll need this password to connect the DataBridge Hub to your network.

### Power the Device

1. **Connect the Power Adapter**

   Plug the included power adapter into the DataBridge Hub, then plug the other end into a wall outlet.

2. **Wait for the Green Light**

   The green LED indicates the device is ready. This may take a few moments.

### Start the Setup in the App

1. **Open the EnergyGrid App**

   Sign in if you haven't already done so.

2. **Go to Toolkit**

   Tap the **Toolkit** icon in the navigation bar.

3. **Select DataBridge Hub**

   Choose the DataBridge Hub from the device options.

4. **Tap Continue**

   The button activates when the app detects the device. Tap **Continue** to proceed.

5. **Tap Begin Setup**

   Follow the on-screen instructions to prepare for pairing.

### Enable Bluetooth

1. **Turn On Bluetooth**

   Enable Bluetooth on your phone or tablet from the device settings or Control Center.

2. **Tap Begin DataBridge Setup**

   The app uses Bluetooth to communicate with the DataBridge Hub during pairing.

### Pair the Device

1. **Place Your Phone Next to the DataBridge Hub**

   Hold your phone directly against the DataBridge Hub as shown in the app.

2. **Wait for the LED to Turn Blue**

   The blue LED indicates that pairing is in progress.

3. **Confirm the Connection**

   A **Success** message appears when pairing completes. Tap **Next** to continue.

> **Tip:** If pairing fails, verify that Bluetooth is enabled and that no other Bluetooth devices are near the DataBridge Hub. Remove your phone case and try again.

### Connect to Wi-Fi

1. **Wait for the Network Scan**

   The app scans for available Wi-Fi networks.

2. **Select Your Network**

   Choose your home Wi-Fi network from the list. Select the 2.4 GHz network for best results.

3. **Enter Your Password**

   Type your Wi-Fi password and tap **Enter**.

4. **Wait for the Connection**

   The app displays a confirmation when the DataBridge Hub connects to Wi-Fi.

> **Note:** Select **Use Wired Ethernet Setup** if you prefer a wired connection instead of Wi-Fi. Select **Enter Network Details Manually** if your Wi-Fi network does not appear in the scanned list.

### Bind to Your Smart Meter

1. **Request the Bind**

   After the Wi-Fi connection succeeds, the app automatically submits a bind request to your smart meter.

2. **Wait for the Process to Complete**

   Binding typically completes within 15 minutes, but may take up to 48 hours depending on network conditions. You do not need to keep the app open or stay near the DataBridge Hub during this time. Keep the device powered and within 30 feet of the smart meter throughout the process.

3. **Check the Status**

   The app displays **Bind Request Complete** when the connection succeeds. Tap **Explore the App** to start using your DataBridge Hub.

> **Note:** If the app still shows the DataBridge Hub searching for the smart meter after 48 hours, contact support through the app.
