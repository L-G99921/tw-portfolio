# Troubleshooting

This guide is intended for utility support agents handling customer inquiries related to BillSense AI.

## BillSense AI Does Not Load

1. **Check Authentication**

    Verify the customer is signed in to the Utility Portal.

2. **Allow Pop-ups and JavaScript**

    Confirm the browser allows pop-ups and JavaScript. If the customer is unsure how to change these settings, advise them to consult their browser's help documentation.

3. **Clear the Cache**

    Clear browser cache and cookies, then reload the page. For specific steps, refer the customer to their browser's help documentation.

4. **Try Another Browser**

    Test with a different web browser.

If the issue persists after all steps, escalate using the [Escalation Path](#escalation-path) below.

## Data Does Not Match the Paper Bill

1. **Explain Exclusions**

    BillSense AI excludes taxes, fees, and voluntary program charges from displayed totals.

2. **Note kWh Variance**

    Displayed kWh values are estimates and may not match official metered figures.

3. **Direct to the Bill Statement**

    Advise the customer to review their latest bill statement for the complete, authoritative breakdown.

If the customer remains concerned about data accuracy, escalate using the [Escalation Path](#escalation-path) below.

## Recommendations Seem Inaccurate

1. **Open My Home**

    Navigate to the My Home section in BillSense AI.

2. **Verify Appliances**

    Confirm that the heating, cooling, and cooking selections match the customer's actual appliances.

3. **Update EV Information**

    Confirm EV ownership and charger type if the customer owns an electric vehicle (EV).

4. **Wait for the Update**

    Recommendations refresh after profile changes. Allow up to 24 hours.

If recommendations still appear inaccurate after profile updates, escalate using the [Escalation Path](#escalation-path) below.

## Customer Mentions LED or Connection Issues

BillSense AI is a web-based tool. It does not have physical LED indicators. If the customer mentions LED issues, they may be referring to a different device — such as the SmartLink Plug. See the [SmartLink Plug documentation](/docs/powerlync/powerlync-installation-guide) for details.

## Support

Contact the appropriate support team for BillSense AI issues. The utility support team handles account, billing, and general customer inquiries. The BillSense AI development team handles persistent technical issues with the BillSense AI application itself.

### Contact Information

- **In-app chat:** Available from the Help Center in the Utility Portal
- **Phone:** Contact your utility's customer service line
- **Support hours:** Check the utility website for current hours

### Escalation Path

1. **First-Level Support**

    Use in-app chat or phone support for general questions. The utility support team handles these inquiries.

2. **Technical Issues**

    Report persistent technical issues to the BillSense AI development team through the **Feedback** button in the bottom-right corner of the BillSense AI interface.

3. **Account Issues**

    Direct account-specific problems to the utility support team via the customer service line.
