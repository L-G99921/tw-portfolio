# SmartLink Plug Installation Guide

## Overview

The SmartLink Plug is a compact smart plug and whole-home energy meter. It connects the EnergyGrid App to your utility smart meter and provides real-time energy usage data. The SmartLink Plug also controls compatible mesh smart devices.

### Key Features

- Real-time electricity usage monitoring via the utility smart meter
- Smart device control for compatible mesh home-automation devices (plugs, bulbs, sensors, and thermostats)
- Local energy measurement for devices plugged directly into the SmartLink Plug outlet
- Compact plug-in form factor

### Limitations

- No support for extended-range smart-home devices — use the DataBridge Hub for those
- No built-in automations, schedules, or notifications
- Best results with 5 or fewer connected mesh smart devices

## Before You Begin

Gather these items before starting the installation.

### Requirements

- SmartLink Plug
- EnergyGrid App installed on iOS or Android
- EnergyGrid account credentials (administrator access required)
- Home Wi-Fi network name and password
- Wi-Fi enabled on the mobile device
- Standard wall outlet within 30 feet (9 meters) of the utility smart meter

:::tip
Use the 2.4 GHz network if your router broadcasts separate 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz networks.
:::

### Placement Guidelines

Choose a location that meets all of the following criteria:

- On the same side of the house as the smart meter
- With minimal walls or large appliances between the SmartLink Plug and the meter
- Within 30 feet (9 meters) of the smart meter

:::warning
Do not place the SmartLink Plug directly behind the meter, near metal racks, near electrical panels, or behind large appliances. These locations interfere with the wireless signal.
:::

## Mobile App Setup

Power on the SmartLink Plug first. Then use the EnergyGrid App to connect the device to your account.

### Sign In

1. **Open the App**

    Launch the EnergyGrid App on your mobile device.

2. **Enter Your Credentials**

    Type your EnergyGrid account username and password. Administrator access is required.

### Start Setup

1. **Open the Toolkit**

    Tap the **Toolkit** icon in the navigation bar at the bottom of the screen.

2. **Begin Setup**

    Locate the SmartLink Plug card and tap **Set Up**. Tap **Begin Setup** on the welcome screen.

### QR Code Scanning

1. **Grant Camera Access**

    Tap **Allow** when prompted to grant camera access.

2. **Scan the Code**

    Locate the QR code on the back of the SmartLink Plug. Hold the QR code in front of the phone camera until it comes into focus. A blue checkmark appears when the scan succeeds. The app uses the QR code to identify the device and prepare the Wi-Fi connection.

### Physical Setup

1. **Plug In the Device**

    Insert the SmartLink Plug into a standard wall outlet within 30 feet (9 meters) of the smart meter.

2. **Check the LED**

    Wait for the LED indicator to pulse blue. This indicates the SmartLink Plug is ready to connect.

3. **Continue in the App**

    Tap **Continue** in the app.

:::note
If the LED does not pulse blue, tap **Need Help?** in the top-right corner of the screen for troubleshooting options.
:::

### Wi-Fi Connection

1. **Connect to the SmartLink Network**

    The SmartLink Plug creates a temporary Wi-Fi network to communicate with your phone. A prompt appears asking you to join this network (displayed as "EBX_000-00000X" or similar). Tap **Join**.

2. **Select Your Home Network**

    Choose your home Wi-Fi network from the list. Select the 2.4 GHz network.

3. **Enter the Password**

    Type the Wi-Fi password and tap **Continue**.

### Meter Binding

1. **Submit the Request**

    The app automatically submits a bind request to the utility after the Wi-Fi connection is established. This request authorizes the SmartLink Plug to communicate with the smart meter.

2. **Wait for Processing**

    The utility typically accepts the bind request within 5 minutes. After the utility accepts the bind request, live energy data may take 24–48 hours to appear in the app.

:::caution
Keep the SmartLink Plug within 30 feet of the smart meter and powered on throughout the binding period.
:::

The **Bind Request Complete** screen confirms a successful request.

## Verify Installation

Check these indicators after the utility processes the bind request, which typically takes 24–48 hours.

### Success Indicators

- The SmartLink Plug LED displays a steady state and is not flashing an error pattern
- Real-time energy usage data appears in the EnergyGrid App
- The SmartLink Plug appears as connected in the Toolkit section of the app

## Troubleshooting

### No Connection After 5 Minutes

1. **Check Power**

    Verify the SmartLink Plug is inserted into the outlet and the LED is on.

2. **Improve Placement**

    Move the SmartLink Plug to an outlet closer to the smart meter with fewer walls between them.

3. **Power Cycle**

    Unplug the device, wait 10 seconds, then plug it back in.

4. **Verify Wi-Fi**

    Confirm the home Wi-Fi network is functioning properly.

### Communication Error During Setup

1. **Verify Wi-Fi**

    Confirm that Wi-Fi is enabled on your mobile device and that you are connected to the correct network.

2. **Check Signal Strength**

    Move closer to the SmartLink Plug. Ensure no large appliances or metal objects are between the phone and the device.

3. **Retry**

    Tap **Try Again** to retry the connection.

### LED Not Pulsing Blue

1. **Power Cycle**

    Unplug the device and wait 10 seconds.

2. **Wait for Activation**

    Plug the device back in and wait up to 60 seconds for the LED to activate.

3. **Contact Support**

    Contact your utility support team if the LED still does not pulse blue.
